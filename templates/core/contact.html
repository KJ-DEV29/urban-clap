Frequently Asked Questions

You can donate blood by registering as a donor on our website and scheduling an appointment. You must be at least 18 years old, weigh at least 110 pounds, and be in good health.

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). Platelet donations can be made more frequently, up to 24 times per year.

You can request blood through our website by filling out a blood purchase form, or by calling our emergency hotline for urgent requests.

We maintain inventory of all blood types: A+, A-, B+, B-, AB+, AB-, O+, and O-. Availability may vary, so please check our current inventory or contact us directly.
